Yuzu Health Styles

Retro style components built purely with css. Powers yuzu.health and yuzu.party
Inspired by blocks.css

Just add this stylesheet to your html.
<link rel="stylesheet" href="https://unpkg.com/@yuzu-health/yuzui/dist/all.min.css" />
Since it's pure CSS, it works with Svelte, React, Vue, regular HTML sites etc...
